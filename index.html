About Nipsy

"She didn't set out to write a book—she set out to expose a murder."

Born June 20, 1986, in Atlanta, Georgia, Nipsy grew up in a working-class family with two older brothers. At 38, she's a widow, volleyball coach, and private investigator with a story that demanded to be told.

In December 2002, when Nipsy was just 16, she was pulled over for speeding by Atlanta police officer James Daniels. They fell in love almost immediately. Eight months later, on August 12, 2003, they married.

James was killed 127 days before Nipsy began writing this book. His death, ruled "accidental," led her to discover his secret investigation into what he called "The Speculation Industry"—a digital empire that profits from human suffering.

By day, she coaches high school volleyball. By night, she runs a private investigation business. But her real investigation is into the systems that killed her husband—and are now targeting their teenage son, Ethan.
