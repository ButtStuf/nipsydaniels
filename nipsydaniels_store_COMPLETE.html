Meet Nipsy Daniels

38, Widowed, and Done with Everyone's BS

Born June 20, 1986, in Atlanta, Georgia, Nipsy Daniels is a 38-year-old author, volleyball coach, and private investigator. Her life took a tragic turn when her husband James was murdered.

This event led her to write "(UNCENSORED) LIT! BITCHES GUIDE TO GO VIRAL BEFORE YOUR BEST FRIEND" - a psychological thriller that exposes the dark underbelly of the "Speculation Industry" and how social media algorithms manipulate us to death.

She didn't set out to write a book—she set out to expose a murder.

Nipsy's voice is raw, authentic, and unapologetically real. She says what everyone thinks but is too scared to post. If you're looking for feel-good platitudes, this isn't your book. But if you want the truth about viral culture, you've found your guide.
